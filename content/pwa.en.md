## PWA

Kattila coffeecam is possible to install on your mobile device as a [PWA]("https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/Progressive_web_apps"). Doing so you will never miss a coffee opportunity fumbling to search for the coffeecam online! PWA's are incredibly exdendable interfaces to the end-user's devices, that enable adding features like background running and push notifications.
